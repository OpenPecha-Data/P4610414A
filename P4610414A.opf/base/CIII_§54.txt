The Buddha then said to the young Licchavi Prabhāvyūha,"Prabhāvyūha, go to the Licchavi Vimalakīrti
to inquire about his illness."

Prabhāvyūha replied, "Lord, I am indeed reluctant to go to that good man to inquire about his
illness.Why?Lord, I remember one day, when I was going out of the great city of Vaiśālī,I
met the Licchavi Vimalakīrti coming in.He greeted me, and I then addressed him: ’Householder,
where do you come from?’

He replied, ’I come from the seat of enlightenment.’

I then inquired, ’What is meant by "seat of enlightenment"?’
