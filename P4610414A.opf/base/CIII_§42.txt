The Buddha then said to the venerable ĀnandaĀnanda, go to the Licchavi Vimalakīrti to inquire
about his illness.

Ānanda replied, Lord, I am indeed reluctant to go to that good man to inquire about his illness.Why?Lord,
I remember one day when the body of the Lord manifested some indispositionand
he required some milk; I took the bowl and went to the door of the mansion of a great Brahmān
family.
