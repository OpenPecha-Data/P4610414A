Meanwhile, the Licchavi Vimalakīrti thought to himself,"Mañjuśrī, the crown prince, is coming
here with numerous attendants.Now, may this house be transformed into emptiness!"Then, magically
his house became empty. Even the doorkeeper disappeared.And, except for the invalid’s
couch upon which Vimalakīrti himself was lying, no bed or couch or seat could be seen
anywhere.
