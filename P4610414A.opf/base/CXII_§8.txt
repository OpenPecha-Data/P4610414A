This King Ratnacchattra honored the Tathāgata Bhaiṣajyarāja and his retinue with many excellent
offerings during five short aeons.At the end of this time, the King Ratnacchattra said
to his sons,’Recognizing that during my reign I have worshiped the Tathāgata, in your turn
you also should worship him.’"The thousand princes gave their consent, obeying their father
the king,and all together, during another five short aeons, they honored the Tathāgata
Bhaiṣajyarāja with all sorts of excellent offerings. "
