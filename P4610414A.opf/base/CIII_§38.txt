The Buddha then said to the venerable RāhulaRāhula, go to the Licchavi Vimalakīrti to inquire
about his illness.

Rāhula replied, Lord, I am indeed reluctant to go to that good man to inquire about his illness.Why?Lord,
I remember that one day many young Licchavi gentlemen came to the place where
I was and said to meReverend Rāhula, you are the son of the Lord, and, having renounced
a kingdom of a universal monarch, you have left the world.What are the virtues and benefits
you saw in leaving the world?
