Lord, the noble sons and daughters who will teach and practice this exposition of the Dharmawill
be honored and served by me and my followers.To the villages, towns, cities, states,
kingdoms, and capitals wherein this teaching of the Dharma will be applied, taught, and demonstrated,I
and my followers will come to hear the Dharma.I will inspire the unbelieving
with faith,and I will guarantee my help and protection to those who believe and uphold the
Dharma."
