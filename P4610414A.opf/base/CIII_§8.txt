Therefore, you should teach the Dharma by keeping your mind on this.You should be adept in regard
to the spiritual faculties of living beings.By means of the correct vision of the wisdom-eye,
manifesting the great compassionacknowledging the benevolent activity of the Buddha,
purifying your intentions, understanding the definitive expressions of the Dharmayou
should teach the Dharma in order that the continuity of the Three Jewels may never be interrupted.
