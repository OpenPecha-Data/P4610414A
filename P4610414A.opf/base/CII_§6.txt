He was compatible with ordinary people because he appreciated the excellence of ordinary merits.

He was honored as the Indra among Indras because he showed them the temporality of their lordship.

He was honored as the Brahmā among Brahmās because he showed them the special excellence of
gnosis.

He was honored as the Lokapāla among Lokapālas because he fostered the development of all living
beings.

Thus lived the Licchavi Vimalakīrti in the great city of Vaiśālī, endowed with an infinite knowledge
of skill in liberative techniques.
